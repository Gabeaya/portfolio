Welcome

Desiring more income, I took up a construction job and really enjoyed the fruits of hard labor. Wanting a job closer to home I decided, "Why not just work from home!" I have oddly found a rush from coding as well as comfort in it. With constant dedication to learn code I am eager to extend the breadth of this page. For now, welcome all and enjoy :)
