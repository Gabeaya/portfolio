The Roots: A little about my background

I had lived in California my entire life. With the spring of covid-19, I decided it was time for a fresh start and packed my bags to Washington. I had studied to become a mechanical engineer which soon diverted to an associates in business. Not satisfied with my decision, I dabbled in logo design for friends, family, and referals. Desiring more income, I took up a construction job and really enjoyed the fruits of hard labor. Wanting a job closer to home I decided, "Why not just work from home!" I stumbled across coding as the pay seemed lucrative, but, being I was never an amazing techie, I had always steered clear of the idea. It may be early on but I feel I have found a new home in the field of front-end programming and feel I am primed for the challenges to come.
